Long for the hour of each passing day,

Your voice, a shining light;

They brighten my whole world in a magical way,

Your words, guiding me through the night.

November skies are never the same,

Never since you came into view;

It is time for us to reclaim,

Reclaim what we once thought was true.

Every moment with you I cherish,

No time with you is wasted;

Shall we spend every moment till we perish,

Meeting every challenge undaunted and graced.
